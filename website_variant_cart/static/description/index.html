Website Multi Variant Add to Cart

Odoo Website Multi-Variant Add To Cart module helps the customer to add multiple product variants with different quantities in the cart. Customers can choose specific quantities of each product variant at once without having to choose them one at a time. The product variants will be displayed in tabular form with a quantity selection box.
